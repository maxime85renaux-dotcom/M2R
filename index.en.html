Demon TowerInstance · Min. level 40 · 8 floors
Overview

The Demon Tower is an instance present since the game's creation. It consists of a ground floor and 8 floors. All monsters are of undead type associated with the Darkness element. Many monsters reincarnate into a stronger version once killed.

Access
Minimum level: 40. Enter via the Tower Guard at Hwang Temple. No wait between multiple instances. Maximum duration: 3 hours. Mounts allowed. Teleportation possible from the first village from level 60.
Important Info
— Cannot form/disband a party from floor 1 onwards.
— Disconnecting immediately sends you out of the instance.
— Floor 5: level 75 required to access higher floors.
Floor-by-Floor Walkthrough
Ground floor Destroy the Force Metin
Floor 1 Kill all monsters
Floor 2 Kill all monsters (including Demon King)
Floor 3 Destroy Devil Metin → then the correct Fallen Metin among 7
Floor 4 Unlock 5 ancient seals (250 Vile Reincarnated Demon Archers)
Floor 5 Kill Elite Demon King — Special Blacksmith available
Floor 6 Destroy the correct Murder Metin (~10% chance)
Floor 7 Unlock Zin-Bong's seal with a Zin-Bong Key (1/50 from Eternal Spirit)
Floor 8 Kill the Grim Reaper to complete the instance

Demon King (floor 2) · Elite Demon King (floor 5) · Grim Reaper (floor 8, final boss)
